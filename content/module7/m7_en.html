Moving Forward

This course introduced you to GCDOCS-the federal government's new content management solution designed to ensure consistent record-keeping and management of electronic information across the public service.

Points to Remember

GCDOCS enables a more open, transparent government, where information is...

  • SHARED and ACCESSIBLE
  • USED by others
  • Being MANAGED
  • VALUED as a strategic resource
  • EVERYONE'S RESPONSIBLITY
