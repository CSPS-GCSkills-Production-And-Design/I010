Easing the pains of information management

Pain Point: Managing who can read or modify documents

Sometimes, Barbara wants to send a 'read-only copy' of a document that she's created to certain individuals. At the same time, she wants her close working team to be able to add comments or edit the document.

In GCDOCS, the owner of the document (the one who saves it to the system) usually manages its Permissions. Permissions are rules that determine what users can see and do with the information. By using the Permissions feature, Barbara can 'update' or modify existing Permissions to give others a certain type of access. In this case Barbara would grant See Contents permissions to those who require ‘read-only’ access.
