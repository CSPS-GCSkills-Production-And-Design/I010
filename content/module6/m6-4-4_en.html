Easing the pains of information management

Pain Point: Interpreting search results

Much of Barbara's time is spent conducting searches and then trying to make sense of the results. Often, she opens and browses multiple documents before finding the one most relevant to her needs.

If Barbara conducts a search in GCDOCS, the search results provide a Relevancy score for each document that's retrieved. The score, expressed as a percentage, indicates how closely the results match her search criteria. The higher the score the more relevant the item.
