Overview: Sharing and Collaborating

This module looks at how you can manage the versions of documents effectively—avoiding the confusion about which version is the latest.

This module also demonstrates how emailing links to documents (rather than attachments) further reduces the confusion and also eliminates the unnecessary duplication of documents.

After completing the module, you will be able to:

  • Manage versioning
  • Email links to documents

When you complete this module, you will have been acquainted with the fundamental features and functions of GCDOCS. You will be ready to get started with the system—to help initiate a new information culture in the Federal Public Service.
