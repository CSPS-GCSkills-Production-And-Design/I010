Easing the pains of information management

Pain Point: Finding the right information, at the right time

Each of the following pages describes a 'pain point' for Barbara when managing information. Your role is to suggest a feature(s) of GCDOCS that might help ease the pain. Type your suggestion in the space provided and press Ask an Expert. Following this, an expert will appear and you can compare your advice with his.

No matter how hard Barbara works at organizing the file and folder structure in her current computer system, she still has difficulty finding important business documents.

Key business documents that she wants to access and share with others in her agency are mixed with other documents that have little to no value (such as copies of old meeting agendas and resumes of employees who have long since left the organization).

In GCDOCS, Barbara can save her business related information in the Enterprise Workspace where it can be easily accessed and shared with others. The Enterprise Workspace in GCDOCS has a standardized folder structure that will remain stable over time. This will make it easy for Barbara to quickly find the information she requires. Also, because information stored in the Enterprise Workspace is regularly reviewed by her local records manager, Barbara will not have to worry about outdated information showing up in her searches. By using GCDOCS, Barbara will have access to the right information, at the right time.
