Easing the pains of information management

Pain Point: Quickly accessing frequently used items

When Barbara is involved in a big project, there are key documents that she refers to time and time again. Barbara wants a way to set some documents apart from other items, making them easily accessible.

Using Favourites would make Barbara's life easier. It provides a quick way to access important documents and items of interest. Adding an item as a Favorite sets it apart from other items in GCDOCS and allows Barbara to access her important information with one click of the mouse.
