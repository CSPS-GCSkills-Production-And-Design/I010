Easing the pains of information management

Pain Point: Identifying the latest version of a document

How many times has Barbara edited a document only to find that—several hours later— she's edited an old version! How many times has she discovered her team members all working with different drafts!

The Version Control feature of GCDOCS will make Barbara's life much easier. She can track who created the document, when it was created, and the version number. She could even Lock versions to ensure that they are not deleted, or purge older ones to avoid confusion.

Emailing a link—rather than sending the document as an attachment—also helps Barbara ensure recipients have the latest version.
