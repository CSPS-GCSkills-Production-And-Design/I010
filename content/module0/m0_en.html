Welcome to GCDOCS Fundamentals: Online Self-paced

This course introduces public servants to GCDOCS—the federal government's new content management solution, designed to ensure consistent record-keeping and management of electronic information across the public service. The system is being deployed as a part of the Open Government initiative and is significantly changing the ways public servants manage information.

In this course, you will learn how to use GCDOCS to improve the way you access, share, and use digital information.

By the end of this course, you will…

  • Recognize the information management challenges facing your organization.
  • Understand how GCDOCS can benefit you, your organization, and the
    Canadian public.
  • Be able to describe the key features and functions of GCDOCS.
  • Be able to demonstrate the fundamental skills necessary to begin using
    GCDOCS effectively.

Course Structure

  • This course has six modules, followed by a final quiz. Each module will likely take
    between 30 to 40 minutes to complete. Passing a 15-question final quiz is required
    for course completion.
  • The modules contain simulations of the GCDOCS system. The "How-to" simulations
    provide step-by-step demonstrations of the fundamental skills you will need to
    use the system. Following most demonstrations, you will have opportunity to "Try it"
    and practice doing these procedures yourself.

Action Planning

The Action Plan is an important feature of this course. The purpose of the Action Plan is to encourage you to reflect on the ways you might use GCDOCS in your daily work and to begin creating a plan for doing so.

In modules 3, 5 and 6 of this course, you will be asked to begin planning actions to help you get started using GCDOCS.

How to Use this Course

  • Schedule approximately 30 to 40 minutes to complete each module. It is advised that you complete only one module at a sitting. Be sure that you've mastered the concepts and skills in each module before moving to the next.
  • This course can be thought of as a continuous learning tool. Meaning, the material will be available to you after you complete the course. You can return to the course again and again to review the demonstrations, to practice a procedure, or access the course resources.
  • Before you begin, take a few minutes to become familiar with the Help section of this course. The Help section provides useful information on:
    • How to navigate the course
    • How to use the demonstrations and simulations
  • The Help section on "How to navigate the course" also introduces you to important course features, including: the Glossary, Quick Reference Card, and links to resources.

We wish you a successful journey learning and benefitting from GCDOCS.
