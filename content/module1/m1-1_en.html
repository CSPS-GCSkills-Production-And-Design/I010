What is GCDOCS?

The collective mind of your organization

GCDOCS is a new tool that will simplify your work life by allowing you to easily save, find, and share information across your organization.

GCDOCS will help you manage your records and documents giving you a single, secure, and organized location for all the information you create and use (e.g., documents, emails, PDFs, images, faxes, and scanned records). It gives you access to the "collective mind" of your organization—ensuring that everyone can access the right information at the right time.

Our changing information culture

Working with shared computer drives, personal drives, and on your desktop is becoming a thing of the past. These older platforms for storing information have a silo effect, encouraging hoarding and massive duplication of information. New systems, like GCDOCS, are changing all of this—enabling better collaboration and sharing and revolutionizing the way we work with information.

When you use GCDOCS you will be helping to create a new norm—a new information culture in our government.
