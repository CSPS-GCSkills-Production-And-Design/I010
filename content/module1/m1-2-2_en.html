Why use GCDOCS?

Overcoming the pain points

A survey of government employees identified these as some of our most common 'pain points' relating to information management.

I can't find anything when I need to!

GCDOCS is a single location for all information, eliminating the need to search in several areas for key information. Powerful search capabilities also significantly decrease search times.

How can I tell if this is the final version?

Versioning lets you immediately identify the latest version of a document.

You call this a file plan?

With GCDOCS you can avoid cumbersome file plans. Using 'favourites' you can personalize how you retrieve information.

My inbox is so full I can't find the important ones!

Email can be moved out of your inbox and into GCDOCS, freeing up your email account and ensuring emails with business value are accessible to others.

Hasn't that file already been created?

Emailing a link instead of an attachment reduces duplication, enhances collaboration, and offers a more efficient way of working.
