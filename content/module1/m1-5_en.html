Key concepts and skills

This module introduced you to GCDOCS. Here are the key concepts to remember:

What is GCDOCS?

  • GCDOCS is a new records and document management system that is being implemented government-wide.
  • It is a single, secure, and organized location for all the information you create and use.
  • Like a 'collective mind,' it allows you to save, find, and share information within your organization across all locations throughout Canada.

Why is it being implemented?

  • Older systems for storing information have led to a silo effectâ€”encouraging hoarding and duplication of information. GCDOCS fosters greater collaboration and sharing and paves the way for a new information culture in government.
  • A key goal of GCDOCS is to promote a more effective government by ensuring that information is: strategically managed, more accessible, better utilized, and easier to locate.
  • GCDOCS supports Canada's Action Plan on Open Government which has been launched to foster greater openness, accountability, and responsiveness.

How will you benefit?

  • In our digital age, we are overloaded with information that grows daily. Yet as a public servant, we are legally obligated to manage all this information. GCDOCS is designed to make this task easier.
  • Among other things, the system helps you: find information you need more quickly, easily identify final versions of documents, create more personalized filing plans, manage your email better, and avoid the needless duplication of information.
  • Most important, GCDOCS helps you share information and collaborate more easily with others across your organization anywhere in Canada.
