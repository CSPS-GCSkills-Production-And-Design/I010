Organizing the messy desk

Does your desk look like this?

This is a good representation of our current way of working with information. We are often swamped with information—unable to find the information we need.

And in the digital age we live in, these challenges are likely to get worse.

The amount of information that we manage daily is growing exponentially. Email alone allows for massive duplication through attachments. What's more, new forms of information continue to add to the volume—wikis, discussion threads, instant messaging, blogs, e-docs and more.

In our daily work life, we are being overloaded with information. The result: duplication of efforts, time and money wasted… greater stress in the workplace.

Yet, as public servants we are legally obligated to manage all of this information.

So, how do we do it?
