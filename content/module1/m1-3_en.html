Promote a more open government

Canada’s Action Plan on Open Government

By now you can see how this new initiative can make your job a whole lot easier. In the bigger picture, however, GCDOCS plays a key role in the Canadian government’s strategy to promote the free flow of information throughout all aspects of government service.

Called Canada’s Action Plan on Open Government, this strategy is designed to foster greater openness, accountability, and a more effective government. GCDOCS has been launched as a government-wide recordkeeping system to support this commitment. GCDOCS is designed to promote more effective information management and utilization and to support—among other things—more informed decision-making and more effective service delivery.

Ultimately it is hoped that these efficiencies will drive innovation and economic opportunities for all Canadians and, at the same time, create a more efficient and responsive government.
