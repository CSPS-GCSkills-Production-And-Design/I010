Overview: Introduction to GCDOCS

This module introduces you to GCDOCS—the new government-wide records and document management system.

GCDOCS will allow you to work more effectively by helping you to access, manage, and share information.

In this first module, you will learn what GCDOCS is and how it fosters a way of working with information that encourages openness and transparency. You will also learn the key benefits—how this tool will help you better manage the information that comes across your desktop.

  • explain why it is being implemented
