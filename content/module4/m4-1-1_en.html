Conducting a Quick Search

More about the Quick Search function

The Quick Search function searches document titles as well as the content of items.

In Marc's case, Quick Search would have likely located the document containing an appendix with tips for time management.

No matter where you are in GCDOCS, the Quick Search function is always available to you. It searches the location where you currently are in the system.

For example, if you are viewing the entire folder structure within the Enterprise Workspace, Quick Search will search the entire Enterprise Workspace. However, if you are viewing a specific folder, Quick Search will just search the contents of that folder. Quick Search also permits you to use operators between keywords such as: "and," "or," or "not."

 

While items in your Personal Workspace are not generally accessible to others, they are still subject to the rules governing Access to Information, Privacy, and legal actions.
