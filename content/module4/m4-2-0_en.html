Interpreting Search Results

Search Results

Once Marc has conducted his search for "tips on time management," a Search Results page is displayed.

As shown here, Marc's search has located two documents containing some or all of his keywords. To interpret these results, Marc looks at the Relevance score for each document.

The Relevance score, represented by a percentage (%), tells Marc how relevant the item is to his keywords or search criteria (i.e., tips on time management).

The first document is 79% relevant and the second document is 73% relevant. Marc plans to have a closer look at the first document, "CSPS Leadership Resources" to see if it contains the tips he is searching for.
