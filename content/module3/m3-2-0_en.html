Using the Personal Workspace

The Personal menu

The Personal menu, in addition to providing access to your Personal Workspace, gives you options for controlling and customizing the way you access or retrieve information in GCDOCS.

The menu's functions help you to easily locate and organize files or folders in the system that are important to you. These might include, for example, tasks assigned to you, documents you have flagged as favourites, or projects that you are working on.
