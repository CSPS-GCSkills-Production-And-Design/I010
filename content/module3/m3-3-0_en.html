What is the Enterprise Workspace?

The Enterprise menu allows you to access the Enterprise Workspace.

The Enterprise Workspace is a location in GCDOCS which serves as your organization's official repository for business information. It is similar to a 'shared drive.' This is the location where your business unit—and your organization at large—can store and access business related information.

When you select Workspace on the Enterprise menu, these shared folders and documents are displayed in the centre of the page. Unlike your Personal Workspace, these are documents that are accessible to others.
