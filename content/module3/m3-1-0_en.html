What is the Personal Workspace?

My Workspace

The Personal menu allows you to access My Workspace.

When you select My Workspace, your own personal folder structure will be displayed in the centre of the page. My Workspace is an ideal location to store GCDOCS items that are specific to you, such as your saved searches and collections.

By default there are only two people who have access to your Personal Workspace, you and the system administrator. Therefore, it is a best practice to always save information related to your work in the Enterprise Workspace. This will ensure business information is accessible to others.

While items in your Personal Workspace are not generally accessible to others, they are still subject to the rules governing Access to Information, Privacy, and legal actions.
