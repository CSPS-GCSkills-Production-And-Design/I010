Locating and Sharing Documents
Gilles, Operations Engineer (CSA)

“For me, locating documents has always been frustrating. It’s about as much fun as a root canal.”

“Quite frankly, I am not the most organized when it comes to filing and sharing documents. But the GCDOCS filing system makes it much easier to retrieve your documents. I find the GCDOCS search engine is really powerful.”

“For example, working with others in GCDOCS, forces me to be more organized. So it might take a bit longer at the beginning. However, in return, I save a lot of time because I can locate things quickly and more easily share my work.”

Collaborating at a Distance; Sharing Documents
Melanie, Learning Advisor (CSPS)

“GCDOCS makes life a whole lot easier when your work group is spread across the country. I am working on a project with another team in my department. We are located in two different provinces with additional team members spread out across the NCR. GCDOCS allows us to work efficiently from our respective locations.”

“Since we are working on a pilot project, we are constantly creating new documents and revising them. Unlike shared drives, I know exactly where to find what I need and know that I am always working with the most recent version.”

Wiki Tool
Sophie, Engineer (CSA)

“The Wiki is a great collaborative tool. Anybody can contribute and if errors pop up, they are easily corrected. In this business where we have to exchange living documents of overdesigned systems, it’s not often easy to summarize what a system does in the real world.”

“The Wiki culture, being encyclopedic in nature, enables the community to share high-level information. This is an enormous advantage for many of our projects. For example, for some projects we might have to integrate systems from different companies across Canada, not all of which interact very well together.”

Improved Productivity
Mario, Project Manager (CSPS)

“How annoying is it when someone keeps asking you for the same document you have already sent them five times?”

“I work on a lot of presentations and reports that undergo constant change. I love the fact that I can send my boss or colleagues a link to my work so they have access to the most recent version. Otherwise, I have to keep re-sending large files, which is a waste of time and server space—not to mention the confusion that results when I send the wrong version. GCDOCS helps me to keep everything organized and updated. I really appreciate the “self-serve” model – I don’t have to keep asking my colleagues where things are and vice versa.”

Standardization and Centralization
Kristina, Systems Engineer (CSA)

“For me the long-term benefit of GCDOCS is the common classification standard, which brings a measure of coherence to the chaos. The organization could not function without this standardization.”

“GCDOCS helps preserve corporate memory in a standardized way. New employees joining a project can quickly find and review past decisions. New projects being initiated can benefit from lessons learned from past ones. And project managers can now track and control progress.”
