Key concepts and skills

In this module you learned about the Personal and Enterprise Workspacesâ€”two important areas of GCDOCS. Here are some key concepts to remember:

Personal Workspace (My Workspace)

  • This is where you store items specific to you. Although the items you store here are not shared, they are still subject to rules governing Access to Information, Privacy, and legal actions.
  • My Workspace is accessed through the Personal menu on the main menu bar.
  • Other functions on the Personal menu help you to easily locate and organize files and folders that are of relevance to you. For example:
    • Collections allows you to create groups of items to share with co-workers.
    • Favourites allows you to designate an item as a "favorite" which sets it apart from other items and makes it easier to find.
    • My Profile can be used to create a personal profile that might include your photo and other information about you.

Enterprise Workspace

  • This location in GCDOCS is where your business unit, or your organization at large, stores and accesses business information. It serves as your organization's official repository for business information.
  • The Enterprise Workspace is accessed through the Enterprise menu on the main menu bar.
  • Users & Groups is another useful feature accessible through the Enterprise menu. This feature helps you to easily find users and groups within your organization. It can be used as a company directory.
