Overview: Using Personal and Enterprise Workspaces

In this module, you will learn about the Personal and Enterprise Workspaces in GCDOCS. The Personal Workspace is like your own desktop within GCDOCS. The Enterprise Workspace is where you share information and collaborate with your colleagues.

After completing the module, you will be able to:

  • describe what the Personal and Enterprise Workspaces are and how they are used
  • access the Personal and Enterprise Workspaces
  • perform some basic operations using these Workspaces
  • develop ideas for using the Workspaces in your daily work
