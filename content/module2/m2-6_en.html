Key concepts and skills

This module acquainted you with GCDOCS. Here is a review of some of the things you should know to get started:

How to launch GCDOCS

  • On the log-in page, enter your User name and Password and press Sign in. This will launch your default start page.

Key areas of the interface

  • Menu Bar: to access your Enterprise workspace, Personal workspace and the Tools menu.
  • Help button: to access help with all features.
  • Content Filter: to sort items displayed on your screen.
  • Breadcrumbs: to identify your current location.
  • Folder and Document Tools: to access folders and documents and manipulate them.
  • Search: to quickly find the items you need.

How to adjust your personal settings

  • Using the Settings function in GCDOCS, you can personalize some of the system's features (e.g. change the colour scheme, language, or default start page). Settings is accessed in the Tools menu.

How to log-out

  • Log-out by selecting Log-out in the Tools menu or by closing your browser.
