Creating a Collection

Why create a Collection

Natalie manages the New Hire Orientation program for her agency. It's a complex program that involves groups across her organization. In the past, managing and organizing all information related to the program was a constant challenge. Documents were sometimes difficult to find, poorly titled, or duplicated in different locations.

Recently, however, Natalie has begun to use GCDOCS. She relies on the feature, Collection, to manage her program information more efficiently.

A GCDOCS Collection allows Natalie to group documents that reside in various locations in GCDOCS together. Making it easy for her to access and share important information.

Natalie's "Orientation Workshop" Collection, for example, contains links to all information related to the workshop. This saves Natalie time and effort. Rather than navigating to the various folders that contain the workshop information, she can access her Collection in one simple step.

Once created, Natalie can save her Collection anywhere she chooses. For example, she can save it in her Personal Workspace for personal use or in the Enterprise Workspace to share it with her colleagues.
