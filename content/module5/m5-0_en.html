Overview: Saving and Organizing

This module introduces you to the different ways you can save and organize documents within GCDOCS. You will learn how to perform tasks such as adding a document or email from another application, managing permissions, and grouping documents so you can easily access them.

After completing the module, you will be able to:

  • create and add to a Collection
  • drag and drop a document into GCDOCS
  • add documents and emails to GCDOCS directly from MS Office
  • manage Permissions
