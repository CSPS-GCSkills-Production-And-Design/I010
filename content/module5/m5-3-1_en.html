Managing Permissions

Tony's writing team

Tony has taken the lead on writing a business case for the implementation of a new software system, called SWIFT, to improve data management in his organization.

His writing team consists of individuals from across the organization. Tony plans to draft most of the document, but wants other team members to be able to review, edit, and write portions of it. He wants to make sure that his team has permission to do so.

Tony will act as senior editor and manage the versions.
