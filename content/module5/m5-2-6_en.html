Adding documents and emails to GCDOCS

Adding email using Enterprise Connect

Enterprise Connect also integrates GCDOCS with MS Outlook. This allows you to save emails that capture business information to GCDOCS, thereby ensuring important decisions and transactions are appropriately managed and accessible to others.

If an email contains a series of forwards and/or replies, save the most recent message in the series that includes previous forwards/replies of relevance. Wait until the thread is complete before saving to avoid saving multiple copies on the same subject.
